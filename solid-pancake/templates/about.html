About Solid Panacake

Made by Sharyar Memon

Solid Pancake is a small web app that allows you to scrape your tweets and do some basic sentiment analysis. The utility uses labelled data of over 20,000 tweets with a positive and negative label. It also creates a word cloud of your tweets to show you a nice visual.
